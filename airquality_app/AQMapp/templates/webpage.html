Curious Minds Want to Know

What's the Air Quality Index?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is like a mood ring for the air, telling you how clean or polluted it is.

How does the sensor work?

Our open-source sensor sniffs out the air like a bloodhound on a mission to keep your lungs happy.

Why should I care about air quality?

Breathing clean air is like giving your lungs a spa day, while polluted air is like making them run a marathon in a dust storm.

Can I trust the data?

Our sensor is as reliable as your grandma's secret cookie recipe, but for air quality.
