About Us

At Air Monitoring Data Viewer Application, we are committed to promoting a healthier environment by providing real-time air quality data to the community. Our mission is to raise awareness about rising air pollution levels and empower individuals to make informed decisions for their well-being.

Our Approach

  • Real-Time Monitoring: Our sensors collect data continuously, providing up-to-the-minute air quality information.
  • Interactive Maps: Explore air quality levels in your neighborhood using our interactive maps.
  • Educational Resources: Learn about pollutants, health risks, and ways to reduce your exposure.
  • Community Engagement: We collaborate with local schools, organizations, and policymakers to drive change.
